Cereal Recipe

How to Make a perfect bowl of Cereal

This is the one and only way to make a bowl of cereal, as made known unto me by God himself. If a man does not make cereal this way, I say unto you he is a sinner in the eyes of God. The Lord is merciful, yes, but as He said to Paul, "Verily I say unto thee, if ye make a bowl of cereal in any other way, ye shall be cast out, and thrust down to Hell,"(Matthew 69:420).

Ingredients

  • Milk (Whole is recommended)
  • Your favorite cereal (if it isn't apple jacks you are WRONG)

Steps

  1. Add milk to bowl to desired amount. "If ye pour the milk, and it falls upon the cereal, behold, ye have not found favor with God,"(Luke 69:69)"
  2. Pour in the cereal
